Awards

Our Wines Are Our Pride

The excellence of our wines has been proved by the following awards.

The Best Craft Wine in California

1956

Our Sauvignon Blanc wine was entered for the first time in a competition and immediately received this award. Experts noted the amazing aroma and luxurious taste.

The Most Intense Red Wine

1992

In 1992, in conditions of very tough competition from winemakers in our state, our wine managed to take first place. It was preferred by most critics.

Year's Best Chardonnay

2016

Our Chardonnay got a gold medal at the Wine Corporation Annual Contest. Our wine scored the highest number of points – 99 out of 100 possible.

The Best American Riesling

2022

In 2022, our wine was presented in this competition among all US wine producers for the first time. And right off the bat, it was able to get the highest award.
